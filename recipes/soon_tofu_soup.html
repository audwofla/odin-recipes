Soon Tofu Soup

What is this dish?

Soon tofu soup is a spicy, savory Korean stew made with tofu, broth, and red pepper oil. I would eat this whenever we would go to the local restaurant. Some common ingredients are enoki mushrooms, kimchi, onions, assorted mushrooms, egg, seafood, and beef.

The recipe we are preparing today is one that I worked on with my girlfriend. Because I am vegan, we will be ommiting the meat, seafood, and egg. Let's go on this adventure to eat this spicy, savory dish!

Ingredients

Broth

  • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 pieces of dried kombu
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 3 tbsp soup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp matsolol or msg
  • salt

Red Pepper Oil

  • 3 tbsps of dried red pepper flakes
  • 4 tbsps of cooking oil
  • 1 tbsps of minced garlic
  • 1/2 chopped yellow onion

Add Ins

  • 1 silken tofu
  • 1 bunch of enoki mushrooms
